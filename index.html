How to configure:
1. Remove battery from crystal, reinsert
2. Crystal LED pulses white = config mode (30 seconds)
3. Tap "Connect to Crystal" above
4. Pick your color, name, and group
5. Tap "Save" — crystal remembers your settings
6. After 30 seconds, crystal enters proximity mode
How the crystals work:

Each crystal has a "charge" that slowly drains when you're alone. Over about two days by yourself, your crystal fades from a healthy glow to a faint flicker to darkness. Tragic.

But when you're near your friends' crystals, they charge each other up. The more friends nearby, the faster the charge. The closer they are, the brighter the glow.

Alone: Faint heartbeat in your color, slowly dying.
1 friend nearby: Your crystal wakes up and starts pulsing. Their color slowly bleeds into yours.
More friends: Your crystal cycles through everyone's colors with smooth fades. Brighter, more alive.
Everyone together: All crystals glow bright, rotating through every color in the group, pulsing in perfect sync.

When a friend leaves, their color fades out of your crystal over about 5 minutes — like a lingering memory.

When a friend arrives after you've been alone for a while, your crystal does a quick bright flash — like it's gasping awake.
